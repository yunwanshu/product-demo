Top 3 Blocking Reasons · Product Solutions

TOP 1
28%
License Plate OCR Failure
Blurry night photos, damaged plates, and non-standard plates prevent OCR recognition, blocking tasks at node N4.
Product Solution
Integrate multi-source OCR cross-validation (primary + backup engine), auto-switch when confidence < 80%; if still failing, route to manual entry queue for completion within 48h. Expected to reduce block rate from 28% to 8%.
TOP 2
22%
Unconfigured Billing Agreement
When new routes or carriers are activated but billing rules haven't been configured in time, tasks cannot match at nodes N8/N9.
Product Solution
Auto-trigger an "Agreement Configuration" to-do upon route creation; escalate to manager if unconfigured after 72h. Meanwhile, generate temporary billing rules for unconfigured routes (referencing similar route averages) to prevent backlog.
TOP 3
18%
WMS Voided Task
Tasks marked as voided in WMS (cancelled transfer / duplicate dispatch) are directly intercepted at node N1.
Product Solution
Add WMS status pre-validation at task generation — voided tasks do not enter the reconciliation queue. Also add a "batch cleanup" feature to auto-archive records voided for over 7 days on a regular schedule.
